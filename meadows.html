The Meadows 

            Welcome to the meadows. This is the first biome that you should come across and is the easiest to survive. Use this time to get accustomed
            to the mechanics of the game. Build yourself a basic house, forage/hunt for food, and gather materials to arm yourself. Below is a list
            of resources you should keep an eye out for as you roam about. Make sure to keep the Deer Trohpies, you'll need them to summon the boss.
  • Wood
  • Stone
  • Flint
  • Fine Wood
  • Dandelions
  • Mushrooms
  • Deer Hide
  • Leather Scraps
  • Feathers
  • Raw Meat
  • Deer Trophy
